PawPi Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026
Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Augusto Traversa ("PawPi," "we," "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the PawPi mobile app and related services (the "Service"). PawPi is an all-in-one app for dog owners: a social feed, dog profiles, health tracking, reminders, vet records, training, community, adoption, and a marketplace of pet services and products.

If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use the Service.

1. Who is responsible for your data

The data controller is:

  • Augusto Traversa
  • Matacos y Alborada S/N
  • Contact: augusto@pawpi.info

2. Information we collect

We collect only what the app needs to work. Specifically:

Information you provide

  • Account information — your email address and name (or display name) when you sign up. If you use Sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the basic profile information those services share (name and email, subject to your choices, including Apple's private-relay email).
  • Pet profiles — information you enter about your dogs: name, handle, breed, age, birthday, gender, weight, photos, and notes.
  • Health and medical content — information you choose to record: weight and wellness logs, photo checks, medications, vaccinations, allergies, known conditions, vet notes, lab results, surgeries, documents you upload, and reminders/routines. This content is entered by you for your own tracking.
  • Social content — posts, captions, photos and videos, comments ("barks"), likes ("paws"), follows, pet friendships, forum threads and comments, events and RSVPs, and lost-and-found reports.
  • Marketplace and booking content — bookings, messages with service providers and other owners, reviews, adoption applications, insurance leads/applications, and prescription/refill requests.
  • Support communications — anything you send us when you contact support.

Information collected automatically

  • Location — with your permission, your device's precise location is used for location-based features (nearby places, walks, transport/pet-taxi, events, adoption listings sorted by distance, and lost-and-found alerts). You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings; the app continues to work with reduced location features.
  • Photos and camera — with your permission, the app accesses your camera and photo library so you can add pet photos, health-check images, and posts, and save the shareable daily frame.
  • Device and usage data — basic technical information needed to operate and secure the Service (for example, app version and error/diagnostic information).

Payment information

  • When you pay for a service or product, payment is processed by our third-party payment processors (MercadoPago and/or Binance Pay). We do not store full card or wallet credentials on our servers; we receive transaction status and references needed to fulfill your order.

3. How we use your information

We use the information to:

  • create and manage your account and your pets' profiles;
  • provide the features you use (feed, health tracking, reminders, vet records, training, community, adoption, and the services marketplace);
  • enable bookings, payments, messaging, and reviews with service providers;
  • send you reminders and notifications you have set up or enabled;
  • show location-based content when you grant location permission;
  • maintain the security and integrity of the Service and prevent abuse;
  • comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites for advertising. The app declares no tracking in its iOS privacy manifest.

4. Health information disclaimer

PawPi helps you track changes and prepare better conversations with your veterinarian. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace professional veterinary care. Health content you enter is stored to help you organize records and notice patterns. Prescriptions appear in the app only as records issued by a licensed veterinary provider through the Service.

5. How information is shared

We share information only as needed to run the Service:

  • With other users, when you choose to — your public dog profile, posts, and other content you post are visible to other users according to the feature (for example, a public post is public; a private walk is visible only to people you invite). Lost-and-found alerts are visible to other users while active.
  • With service providers you transact with — when you book, message, review, apply to adopt, or request a prescription/insurance, the relevant business receives the information needed to serve you. Sharing of pet health data with a provider happens through explicit in-app consent.
  • With people you invite — family members or caregivers you grant access to, at the scope and for the duration you set; you can revoke access at any time.
  • With processors and infrastructure providers — payment processors (MercadoPago/Binance Pay), our database/hosting provider (Supabase), authentication, and similar vendors that process data on our behalf under contract.
  • For legal reasons — to comply with the law, enforce our terms, or protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the public.

6. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You can delete content within the app, and you can delete your entire account at any time (see Section 8). We may retain limited records where required by law (for example, transaction records).

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at augusto@pawpi.info.

You can also:

  • control camera, photo, location, and notification permissions in your device settings;
  • edit or delete pets, posts, logs, and other content within the app;
  • delete your account in the app (Settings → Delete account).

8. Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account from Settings → Delete account. This irreversibly removes your account and the data you own (your profile, pets, and the records associated with them), subject to limited legal retention. This is separate from logging out or resetting local app data.

9. Children's privacy

PawPi is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live, including where our hosting and processing vendors operate. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including row-level access controls so each user's data is scoped to that user. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and continually improve our safeguards.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date and, for material changes, provide additional notice within the app.

13. Contact us

Questions about this Policy or your data: augusto@pawpi.info, Augusto Traversa, Matacos y Alborada S/N.
