Getting started

  • Create an account — open PawPi and sign up with your email, or use Sign in with Apple or Google. You must be at least 16 to use PawPi.
  • Add your dog — go to More → My Dog to create your dog's profile (name, breed, age, photo, and more). Your dog's information is linked across the app.
  • Set up reminders — open More → Reminders & Routines to create feeding, walk, photo-check, medical-care, wellness-check, and vet-appointment routines. Due items show up under Health → Today.

Common questions

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen, choose "Forgot password" and follow the email instructions. If you signed up with Apple or Google, sign in with that same provider instead of a password.

How do I edit or delete my dog's information?

Open the dog's profile under More → My Dog (social profile) or your private Dog Profile to edit details. Health and medical records live under Health → Vet Record. You can add, edit, or remove records there.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and the data you own (your profile, pets, and associated records), subject to limited legal retention. Deleting your account is different from logging out.

The app is asking for camera, photos, or location — why?

PawPi asks for these only to power features you use: the camera and photo library let you add pet photos, health-check images, and posts; location powers nearby places, walks, events, and lost-and-found alerts. You can grant or revoke any permission at any time in your device's Settings, and the app keeps working with reduced features.

How do bookings and payments work?

When you book a pet service or buy from the shop, payment is handled by our payment processors (MercadoPago and/or Binance Pay). PawPi does not store your full card or wallet credentials.

A note on health features

PawPi helps you track changes and prepare better conversations with your veterinarian. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace professional veterinary care. The health content you enter is stored to help you organize records and notice patterns over time.

Reporting a problem or safety concern

If something isn't working, or you want to report content, a user, or a safety concern, email augusto@pawpi.info with the details and we'll look into it.
